Automatic Short Answer Question Generation

Developed a system utilizing Bloom's Taxonomy for automatic short answer question generation. The system generates questions, prompts users for answers, and evaluates the similarity between user-provided answers and model-generated answers. The model assigns individual marks to each question based on the level of similarity.

Online Voting System

Designed and implemented an Online Voting System to streamline and secure the voting process. The system ensures efficient and transparent elections, allowing users to cast their votes remotely while maintaining the integrity and confidentiality of the voting process.

Hotel Management System

Created a comprehensive Hotel Management System to facilitate seamless operations in the hospitality industry. The system includes features for room reservations, check-ins, check-outs, and management of guest information, providing an integrated solution for efficient hotel management.

Education Management System

Developed an Education Management System to optimize administrative tasks in educational institutions. The system includes modules for student enrollment, course management, grading, and communication, streamlining various processes to enhance the overall efficiency of educational administration.
